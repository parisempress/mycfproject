Why did you decide to become a web developer?

Web development became my passion whilst pursuing my degree in Material Chemistry. I was in a computational modelling class and while gaining the experience of working with computers I wanted to delve further into the field of computer science. Thus far it has been a great experience and I am happy that I am able to diversify my career.

What is your design process?

My design process involves planning and determing the aim and vision of the project. Following this I work on the design and development of the project to suit and meet the expectation of the client.

What are your strengths?

My strengths include having a strong work ethic, analytic skills, great interpersonal and communication skills and definitely a strong sense of responsibility
